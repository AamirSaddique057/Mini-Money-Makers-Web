Our Story

Creating little moments that shape big futures

Mini Money Makers began with one mum, a curious toddler, and a simple question: how do we teach little ones about money in a way that feels fun, gentle, and part of everyday life?

I’m Adah, a first-time mum and former financial adviser. When my daughter turned two, I wanted to help her grow up confident with money, but every tool I found felt too serious, too complex, or just not made for children her age. I wanted something playful and cute, something she would naturally reach for. So I decided to create the brand I wished existed.

Research from Cambridge University shows that children begin forming their money habits by the age of seven. That insight changed everything for me. If these habits start early, then the learning should start early too: in a way that feels age-appropriate, calm, and joyful.

Mini Money Makers was created to support parents who want the same. Through stories, characters, flashcards, and simple activities, we make early financial learning warm, accessible, and rooted in play. No pressure. No overwhelm. Just small moments of learning woven into real family life.

Our mission is simple: to nurture financial confidence in young children and empower families to build healthy money habits together, one playful conversation at a time.
